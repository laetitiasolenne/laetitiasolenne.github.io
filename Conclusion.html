Evaluation and improvement

A strength to this investigation was using different values for the systolic and diastolic measurement for an individual whereby the mean values were used thus providing more accurate and reliable results .However the presence of outliers in the different values obtained for both the systolic and diastolic measurement may have affected the reliability of the results obtained. A very high or low BMI therefore influences the average BMI values obtained similarly for the systolic values reason why the linear relationship may not be as accurate as it suppose to be . in order to solve this issue a one by one relationship could be use rather than an average one .
